Foreword ^^

BU küçük kitap (meditasyon ve deneyim sonucudur) üzerine fazlasıyla yazılmış olan düşüncenin gücü konusunda kapsamlı bir inceleme değildir. Açıklayıcı olmak yerine, fikir vericidir. Amacı, erkek ve kadınları şu gerçeğin keşfine ve farkındalığına teşvik etmektir:

"Onlar, kendi kendilerini yapanlardır."

by virtue of the thoughts, which they choose and encourage; that mind is the master-weaver, both of the inner garment of character and the outer garment of circumstance, and that, as they may have hitherto woven in ignorance and pain they may now weave in enlightenment and happiness.

1. Thought and Character ^^

THE aphorism, "As a man thinketh in his heart so is he," not only embraces the whole of a man’s being, but is so comprehensive as to reach out to every condition and circumstance of his life. A man is literally what he thinks, his character being the complete sum of all his thoughts.
